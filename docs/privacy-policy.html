Privacy Policy

PetsWorld Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

PetsWorld provides pet product browsing, ordering, saved items, address management, account authentication, invoice generation, and admin order management.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, phone number, and user ID
  • Saved addresses and delivery details
  • Cart items, saved products, and order history
  • Payment metadata required to confirm payment status
  • Product reviews and basic support or admin activity related to store operations

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process orders, invoices, and delivery details
  • Save your cart, bookmarks, and order history
  • Support login methods including email/password, phone OTP, and Google Sign-In
  • Help store admins manage catalog, banners, and orders
  • Meet legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, and customer-support obligations

Payments

Online payments may be processed through third-party payment providers such as Razorpay. PetsWorld does not store full card details in the app database.

Data Storage

Data may be stored in Firebase services used by the app, including authentication and cloud database services. Product and banner media may be stored using third-party media hosting configured by the store operator.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only with service providers and business tools required to operate the app, process orders, authenticate users, store data, and manage payments.

Account Deletion

Users can request deletion directly inside the app from the Profile screen. Deleting an account removes the user profile, saved items, cart items, addresses, and order records associated with that account, subject to any legal retention duties the store operator may have.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children

This app is not intended for children to make purchases without supervision.
